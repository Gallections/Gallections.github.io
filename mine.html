Game Rules

  • Each cell can be treated as the center of a 3 X 3 square, the number on the center of the cell represents the number of mines that is hidden in the 8 cells surrounding the center cell.
  • For the cells on the corners or edges, the number would represent the number of mines that is hidden in the rest of the cells surroudning that cell.
  • To help marking the position of mine, right click to add a flag on the cell.
  • To win the game, you need to reveal all the non-mined cells. If a mine is left-clicked, you lose the game!
  • Happy Mining! ;)
